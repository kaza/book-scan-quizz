About AI-Quizzer

Welcome to AI-Quizzer, an innovative learning application that harnesses the power of Google's Gemini AI to transform the way you study and learn. Our mission is to create an intelligent, adaptive, and engaging learning experience that caters to diverse learning styles and needs.

AI-Quizzer was inspired by observations in Austria of the challenges faced by immigrants and refugees from various regions affected by conflicts and crises. As educational systems, including Austria's, strive to provide sufficient resources to these diverse groups, AI can step in to bridge the gap. By allowing users to generate quizzes and receive language feedback from a simple photo of a book page, AI-Quizzer offers an innovative solution for learning both the local language and school subjects.

We believe that AI can provide affordable, accessible support to every child, enabling them to catch up at their own pace without anxiety. Our goal is to empower students from all backgrounds to integrate more effectively into their new educational environments, breaking down barriers of language and knowledge. With AI-Quizzer, we're turning the challenges of adaptation and integration into opportunities for personalized, efficient learning.

Key Features

Intelligent Quiz Generation

  • Snap photos of book pages and watch as Gemini AI creates relevant, accurate questions based on the content.
  • Ensures quiz material is directly aligned with your study material, no hallucinations guaranteed

AI-Powered Feedback

  • Gemini AI analyzes your responses, providing detailed, constructive feedback to enhance your understanding.
  • Feedback can be displayed on-screen or read aloud, adapting to your preferences.

Conversational Language Learning

  • Practice speaking in your target language with AI-generated conversation prompts.
  • Receive instant feedback on grammar, pronunciation, and content relevance.

Multi-Modal Learning Support

  • Text-to-Speech: Questions can be read aloud in multiple languages, supporting auditory learners and improving accessibility.
  • Speech-to-Text: Record your answers or use real-time dictation for a hands-free learning experience.

Adaptive Technology

  • Choose between on-device AI for quick, low-bandwidth use or cloud-based AI for enhanced capabilities.
  • Seamlessly switch between text, and speech inputs to suit your learning environment.
