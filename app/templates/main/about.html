About Book-Scan-Quizz

Welcome to Book-Scan-Quizz, the AI-powered app revolutionizing how we learn!

Our Mission

At Book-Scan-Quizz, we're on a mission to make learning more accessible, engaging, and efficient for everyone. Whether you're a student, teacher, lifelong learner, or language enthusiast, our app is designed to enhance your learning experience.

Powered by Google Gemini API

Book-Scan-Quizz harnesses the power of Google's Gemini API to deliver a cutting-edge learning experience. This advanced AI technology allows us to offer features that are both innovative and highly effective.

Key Features

1. Instant Quiz Generation

Transform any book into an interactive quiz! Simply snap photos of book pages, and our AI generates multiple questions based on the content. The best part? Answers are sourced directly from the book, ensuring accuracy and relevance.

2. Multilingual Text-to-Speech

Our on-device AI brings questions to life with natural-sounding speech in multiple languages. Perfect for auditory learners or anyone who prefers a more natural learning flow.

3. Audio Answer Evaluation

Record your answers verbally, and let our Gemini AI analyze them. Receive detailed feedback on your responses, including completeness and correctness grades.

4. On-Device Speech-to-Text

Even with low bandwidth, you can dictate your answers using our on-device speech-to-text feature. Your spoken words are converted to text in real-time, right on your device, ensuring a smooth experience regardless of internet connectivity.

5. Comprehensive Performance Review

After completing a quiz, review your performance with our easy-to-understand summary. Track your progress and identify areas for improvement.

6. Easy Sharing and Collaboration

Share quizzes or individual questions with friends, classmates, or students. Foster a collaborative learning environment and challenge each other to grow.

Why Choose Book-Scan-Quizz?

  • Accessibility: Features like text-to-speech and speech-to-text make learning accessible to everyone, including those with visual impairments or reading difficulties.
  • Efficiency: Generate quizzes instantly from any book, saving time and effort in creating study materials.
  • Flexibility: Learn at your own pace, in your preferred language, using either audio or text-based methods.
  • Accuracy: With questions and answers sourced directly from books, you can trust the content you're learning.
  • Innovation: Cutting-edge AI technology ensures you're using the most advanced learning tools available.
  • Privacy: On-device processing for speech features ensures your data stays private.
