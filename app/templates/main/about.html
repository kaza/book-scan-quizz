Welcome to AI-Quizer, an innovative learning application that harnesses the power of Google's Gemini AI to transform the way you study and learn. Our mission is to create an intelligent, adaptive, and engaging learning experience that caters to diverse learning styles and needs.

Key Features

1. Intelligent Quiz Generation

  • Snap photos of book pages and watch as Gemini AI creates relevant, accurate questions based on the content.
  • Ensures quiz material is directly aligned with your study material.

2. Multi-Modal Learning Support

  • Text-to-Speech: Questions can be read aloud in multiple languages, supporting auditory learners and improving accessibility.
  • Speech-to-Text: Record your answers or use real-time dictation for a hands-free learning experience.

3. AI-Powered Feedback

  • Gemini AI analyzes your responses, providing detailed, constructive feedback to enhance your understanding.
  • Feedback can be displayed on-screen or read aloud, adapting to your preferences.

4. Conversational Language Learning

  • Practice speaking in your target language with AI-generated conversation prompts.
  • Receive instant feedback on grammar, pronunciation, and content relevance.

5. Adaptive Technology

  • Choose between on-device AI for quick, offline use or cloud-based AI for enhanced capabilities.
  • Seamlessly switch between text, audio, and speech inputs to suit your learning environment.
