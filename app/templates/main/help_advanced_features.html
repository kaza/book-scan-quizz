Additional Features

Quiz Sharing

AI-Quizzer allows you to share your quizzes with others in two ways:

Sharing from "My Quizzes" page:

  1. Go to the "My Quizzes" page.
  2. Find the quiz you want to share.
  3. Click the "Share" button next to the quiz.
  4. A shareable link will be generated and copied to your clipboard.
  5. You can now paste this link and send it to others.
  6. Anyone with the link can access and take the quiz.

Sharing from "My Sessions" page:

  1. Navigate to the "My Sessions" page.
  2. Locate the quiz session you want to share.
  3. Click the "Share Quiz" button associated with that session.
  4. The shareable link for the quiz will be copied to your clipboard.
  5. You can then paste and share this link with others.

Progress Tracking and Performance Analytics

Track your progress and view performance analytics:

  1. Navigate to the "My Sessions" page from the main menu.
  2. Here, you'll see a list of all your quiz sessions, including:
    • Quiz title
    • Start time
    • Status (in progress or completed)
    • Progress bar showing percentage of questions answered
  3. Click on a completed session to view detailed results.
  4. For ongoing sessions, click "Continue" to resume the quiz.

Scoring System Explanation

AI-Quizzer uses a comprehensive scoring system to evaluate your answers:

  • Correctness Score: Ranges from 0 to 10
    • Measures how accurate your answer is compared to the correct answer
    • A higher score indicates a more accurate response
  • Completeness Score: Ranges from 0 to 10
    • Indicates how thorough your answer is
    • A higher score means you've covered more aspects of the expected answer
  • Language Skills (for language practice): Ranges from 0 to 10 for each aspect
    • Pronunciation: Evaluates the accuracy of your spoken language
    • Grammar: Assesses the correctness of your sentence structure and word usage
    • Content: Measures the relevance and appropriateness of your response
  • Scores are provided for each question you answer.
  • At the end of a quiz session, you can review these scores for each question.
  • The AI provides feedback explaining why you received each score, helping you understand areas for improvement.

Remember, the goal is not just to get high scores, but to learn and improve. Use the feedback provided with each score to enhance your understanding of the subject matter and language skills.
