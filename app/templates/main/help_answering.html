Answering Questions

AI-Quizzer offers two modes for answering questions: Audio Mode and Text Mode. Here's how to use each:

Audio Mode

  1. Activate Audio Mode: Toggle the switch to "Audio" if not already selected.
  2. Start Recording: Click and hold the microphone button to begin recording your answer.
  3. Speak Clearly: Enunciate your answer clearly into your device's microphone.
  4. Stop Recording: Release the microphone button when you've finished answering.
  5. Review and Submit: Your answer will be transcribed. Review it and click "Submit" if you're satisfied, or re-record if needed.

Text Mode

  1. Activate Text Mode: Toggle the switch to "Text" if not already selected.
  2. Type Your Answer: Use the provided text box to type out your answer.
  3. Review Your Answer: Double-check your typed response for any errors.
  4. Submit: Click the "Submit" button to send your answer for evaluation.

Switching Between Modes

  • Use the toggle switch above the answer area to switch between Audio and Text modes at any time during the quiz.
  • Your progress is saved, so you can switch modes without losing your place in the quiz.

Receiving Feedback

  • After submitting your answer, you'll receive feedback on your response.
  • Review the feedback carefully to understand areas for improvement.
  • If TTS is enabled, the feedback will be read aloud to you.

Remember, practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if you don't get everything right the first time. Use the feedback to improve your knowledge and skills.
