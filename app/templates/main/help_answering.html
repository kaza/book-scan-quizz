Answering Questions

AI-Quizzer offers two modes for answering questions: Audio Mode and Text Mode. You can switch between these modes at any time using the toggle button above the answer area.

Audio Mode

  1. Click the "Record" button to start recording your answer.
  2. Speak your answer clearly into your device's microphone.
  3. Release the "Record" button when you're finished.
  4. Your audio is uploaded and analyzed by cloud AI.
  5. The AI compares your audio recording against the question and original answer.
  6. Receive detailed evaluation and friendly feedback on your response, including scores for correctness and completeness.

Text Mode

  1. Toggle to "Text Mode" if not already selected.
  2. Click and hold the "Push to Answer" button to start recording your answer.
  3. Speak your answer (on-device AI converts speech to text).
  4. The system uses the language selected in your text-to-speech settings.
  5. You'll see your text appear in the "Transcribed Text" area as you speak.
  6. Release the button to send your answer for evaluation.
  7. Gemini AI evaluates your text response, comparing it to the original question and answer.

Feedback and Evaluation

  • Feedback is provided on-screen for both audio and text responses.
  • The feedback can be read aloud if TTS is enabled.
  • You'll receive correctness and completeness scores based on your answer's alignment with the original question and correct answer.

Language Conversation Practice

  1. The target language for practice is specified when creating a quiz.
  2. Read or listen to the conversation prompt.
  3. Respond in the target language using audio only (Text mode is not available for language practice).
  4. Gemini Multimodal AI evaluates your response, considering:
    • Grammar
    • Pronunciation
    • Content relevance
  5. Receive feedback in your native language, including friendly suggestions for improvement.

Whether you're practicing specific questions or engaging in language conversations, AI-Quizzer provides a comprehensive, AI-powered learning experience to help you improve your skills.

Future Enhancements

We plan to integrate advanced AI models for text-to-speech and speech recognition as they become available through Chrome's built-in AI capabilities. This will enhance our voice interaction features without server-side processing, providing a smoother and more efficient user experience.
