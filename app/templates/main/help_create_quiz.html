Creating a Quiz

To create a quiz in AI-Quizzer, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the "Create Quiz" section from the main menu.
  2. Choose between "Language" or "Questions" quiz type:
    • Language: For practicing conversations and language skills.
    • Questions: For traditional question-and-answer format quizzes.
  3. For "Questions" type:
    • Upload images of your study material, or
    • Manually enter questions and answers.
  4. For "Language" type:
    • Set up conversation prompts or topics.
    • Specify the target language for practice.
  5. Give your quiz a title and save it.
  6. Start practicing with your newly created quiz!
