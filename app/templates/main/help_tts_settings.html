TTS (Text-to-Speech) Settings

Customize your Text-to-Speech experience in AI-Quizzer:

  • Select TTS Language:
    • Click on the language dropdown in the TTS settings panel.
    • Choose your preferred language for text-to-speech output.
    • This setting affects the voice used for reading questions and feedback.
  • Auto-Read Feature:
    • Find the "Automatically read results aloud" checkbox in the TTS settings.
    • Check this box to have questions and feedback automatically read out loud.
    • Uncheck it if you prefer to manually trigger speech playback.
  • Adjust Speech Rate:
    • Locate the speech rate slider in the TTS settings.
    • Move the slider left for slower speech or right for faster speech.
    • Find a comfortable speed that allows you to understand the content clearly.
  • Test TTS Settings:
    • After adjusting settings, use the "Test" button to hear a sample.
    • Make further adjustments as needed for your comfort and understanding.

Remember, you can always adjust these settings during a quiz session to suit your needs.
