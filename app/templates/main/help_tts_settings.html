TTS (Text-to-Speech) Settings

AI-Quizzer uses advanced Text-to-Speech technology to read questions and results aloud. Here's how to customize your experience:

Selecting TTS Voices

  • Available voices vary based on your operating system and browser.
  • Google voices offer the highest quality on Chrome devices.
  • To find Google voices:
    1. Open the voice selection dropdown.
    2. Use the filter feature to search for "Google" and your preferred language.

TTS Options

  • On-device AI: Provides quick text-to-speech for immediate feedback.
  • Cloud-AI: Offers enhanced voice quality for a more natural sound.

Customizing TTS Settings

  1. Select Language:
    • Choose your preferred language for TTS output.
    • AI-Quizzer supports multiple languages for questions and feedback.
  2. Adjust Speech Rate:
    • Use the speed slider to set a comfortable pace.
  3. Enable Auto-Read:
    • Toggle the "Automatically read results aloud" option.
    • When enabled, questions and feedback will be read automatically.

These TTS features improve accessibility, aid younger learners, benefit those with visual impairments, and cater to auditory learners.
